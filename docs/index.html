Frequently Asked Questions

How are roofers listed on TopRoofers?
We pull data directly from Google Maps and verify that each business has a real website, a physical address, and customer reviews. Listings are sorted by rating and review count by default, not by who pays the most.
Is it really no cost to be listed?
Yes. Every roofing contractor with a Google Business listing is included at no cost. Claiming your listing is also no cost. Featured placement is available as an optional upgrade.
What is a dofollow backlink and why does it matter?
A dofollow backlink tells Google that TopRoofers is vouching for your website. This passes authority to your site and can help it show up higher in search results. Most large directories (like Yelp) use nofollow links, which pass no value. Ours are dofollow.
How often is the data updated?
We refresh all listing data monthly from Google Maps. Ratings, review counts, and business details are updated automatically. If you claim your listing, you can update your information at any time.
How do I remove my business from this directory?
Contact us and we will remove your listing within 24 hours. No questions asked.
